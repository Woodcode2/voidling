# VOIDLING: Art Swap, People + Vehicles (Rebuild Prompt 4)
Swap the flat people and cars for the new clay sheets, and fix the stiff car movement. The cutter is already proven on the building and house sheets, so this is a faster swap with one real addition: vehicles rotate to face their direction of travel.

## GOAL
Replace the ambient people sprites and the vehicle sprites with cutouts from two new clay sheets, mapped into the existing spawners, and make each vehicle point where it is driving. Keep all movement logic, spawn logic, and gameplay exactly as they are.

## DO NOT TOUCH (leave fully intact)
- Car pathing, junction turning, spawn rates, and people spawn logic. Only the sprite and the vehicle's facing angle change.
- The Prompt 3 building and house swap, the Prompt 1 depth work, and the Prompt 2 juice layers. Reuse them, do not rewrite them.
- Gameplay: eating, vacuum, growth, evolution, predation, scoring, lot density, audits.
- Camera, ground, roads, river, waterfall, void body rendering.
- Use the SAME injection approach `clayCity.ts` already uses: set only the visual `spriteBounds`, never `spriteContactFrac`, so eat-contact radius stays unchanged for people and cars too.

- If a vehicle heading toward the camera looks slightly off when fully rotated, that is acceptable for now and we note it as later polish. The goal here is cars that turn with the road instead of sliding sideways.
