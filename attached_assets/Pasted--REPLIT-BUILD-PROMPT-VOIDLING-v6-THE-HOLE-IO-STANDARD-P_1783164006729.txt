## 1 — Match structure: 3-minute rounds

- Round length becomes **180 seconds**. Power-up picks at **2:30, 1:40, and 0:50** remaining (three picks per round).
- Economy retune: coins earned = floor(score / 150). Daily mission targets scale ×1.5.
- Evolution thresholds (section 3) tuned so a median run peaks at GOBBLER, a good run at DEVOURER, and WORLD EATER lands in roughly the top 10% of runs.

## 2 — Respawn + catch-up (nobody is ever mathematically dead)

- **Trickle respawn:** maintain the world at ≥85% of its initial object population at all times. Respawn T1–T3 objects only (houses and the water tower are finite trophies), always off-screen for the player, weighted toward the sparsest regions, at 2–4 objects/second as needed. The map must never feel eaten-out.
- **Golden objects:** starting at 2:45 remaining, spawn one golden variant every ~12s — any T1–T3 object with a gold tint, sparkle particles, and a soft ping. Worth 3× mass and 3× score. Bots chase them too.
- **Underdog aura (silent):** whoever is in 5th or 6th place gets +12% move speed and +25% growth, with only a faint blue sparkle trail as a tell. No banner, no explanation.
- **Leader decay:** any void above the DEVOURER threshold passively loses 0.4% mass/second (big bodies burn fuel). Decay can never drop a void below the floor of a form it has already reached.

## 3 — EVOLUTIONS (the milestone system — build in full)

Per-round form ladder driven by radius. Forms only go up within a round; being chomped can't remove one.

| Form | Radius | Visual layer gained (permanent for the round) |
|---|---|---|
| VOIDLING | 22 (start) | — |
| MUNCHER | 36 | thin rotating energy ring |
| GOBBLER | 54 | 3 debris chunks orbiting outside the ring |
| DEVOURER | 78 | crown of purple flame licks |
| WORLD EATER | 105 | violet screen-edge warp; background stars bend toward you; nearby objects visibly lean away in fear |

**Transformation moment, every threshold:** world slows to 0.3× for 600ms → white shockwave ring → the void spins once, scaling 1.25→1.0 → **giant Fredoka title card slams in ("GOBBLER!") with a confetti burst (24 pieces)** → fanfare chord → `vibrate([30,40,60])`. Each form grants +8% move speed, stacking.

- HUD: the evolution progress bar under the timer glows as it fills and pops with a flash at each threshold, labeled with the next form's name.
- Leaderboard shows a tiny crown on any WORLD EATER. Results screen shows "Reached: DEVOURER" with the form icon; WORLD EATER results get a special gold treatment and +200 coins. The share card includes the form reached.

**Pickup moment:** on card tap — 0.4s freeze, the card flies into the void, the void flashes the power-up's color with a ring pulse, the name stamps center-screen in Fredoka for 700ms, sound sting, `vibrate(25)`. Active power-up icons stay under the timer with duration rings.

## 5 — WORLD EVENTS (drama + catch-up pressure)

Each event announces with a 2-second warning banner + horn before it starts.

- **GOLDEN RUSH** at 2:05 remaining: for 10 seconds, golden objects rain in around all players (2/second each).
- **SHRINK STORM** at 1:10 remaining: a dark purple cloud with lightning spawns and slowly hunts **the current #1** for 12 seconds; anyone it touches loses 12% mass (never below a reached form's floor). It's escapable — cloud speed is 70% of base player speed.
- **TOWN FIGHTS BACK** — triggered the moment anyone reaches WORLD EATER: two tiny firetrucks chase that void for 8 seconds, spraying water arcs that apply a -15% speed aura while connected. Purely comedic, fully drawn (truck, ladder, water particles). This is a signature clip moment — make it charming.
- Bots react to all events: chase golden spawns, flee the storm, scatter from firetrucks.

## 6 — Bot brain fixes

- **SATED state:** after a bot eats the player OR any rival, it enters SATED for 6 seconds — drifts away from its victim, only grazes, cannot target voids. Kills the instant re-chase problem.
- **Anti-idle:** if a bot has moved less than 80px net over 4 seconds, or hasn't eaten anything for 6 seconds, force-retarget it to the nearest dense object cluster. No bot may camp a corner.
- **Wall avoidance:** steering adds a repulsion vector within 150px of the world edge so bots never hug or jitter against walls.
- Bots respect the respawn economy (they benefit from underdog aura and suffer leader decay identically to the player).

## 7 — World edge: the neighborhood floats in space

- In the final 60px before the world boundary, the ground dissolves into the starfield void with a dithered fade — the town is an island floating in space. On-brand and intentional.
- Every road ends at a striped barrier with a hedge behind it, placed **before** the fade. No road, sidewalk, or lawn may render past the barrier line — nothing may visually continue beyond the playable edge.
- Hitting the boundary: soft bounce, squash wobble on the void, quiet "thunk." The invisible wall sits exactly at the visual edge.

## 8 — Environment art pass (kill the flat-board look)

- **Global lighting:** every object's hard offset shadow lengthens and angles consistently to the southeast (offset 6px x, 8px y) — one sun, one world. Add a very soft warm vignette (3% orange, screen edges).
- **Grain:** a static 2%-opacity noise texture overlay on the ground layer only. This alone removes the "flat game board" feel.
- **Zone grading:** park block grades slightly cooler green; plaza slightly warmer; residential neutral. Subtle, but the map should feel like districts.
- **New props:** backyard pools (T4, blue ellipse + deck), streetlights (T3, edible, lamp glow dot), stop signs (T2), bus stop bench with sign (T3), clotheslines between some houses (decor), a mail truck that drives and flees (T4). Two house sizes for variety; some lots get picket-fenced backyards.
- Puddles get a faint sky-tint highlight. Pond gets 2–3 drawn lily pads and a slow ripple ring.

## 9 — Character: crisper and more alive

- Rebuild the cached body sprite at 3× resolution. Flat fill + darker bottom crescent + one crisp highlight — verify no residual gradient or blur survives anywhere, including the home screen and shop.
- **Expression states:** idle contented smile; mouth opens approaching food; visible chomp; **cheek puff for 400ms after eating anything T3+**; dizzy swirl eyes for 1s after being chomped; tongue-lick grin (drawn, not emoji) for 800ms after a TRIPLE.
- Eyes get a moving sparkle dot; blink timing stays organic; pupils lead into turns.

## 10 — Audio v2 (no more Sonic rings)

- **Eat sound rebuilt:** layered 100ms gulp — a low sine thump (120Hz, 40ms), a band-passed noise "nom" sweeping downward (80ms), and a small triangle blip that carries the pitch ladder. Bigger tiers = deeper thump + a crunch noise burst. It should sound like swallowing, not collecting a coin.
- Merge/TRIPLE: fat triple-pop + warm chord. Evolution: rising fanfare (4 notes). Event warning: two-note horn. Win: tiny synth crowd cheer.
- Music: soften to plucked marimba-style triangle notes with fast decay, 96 BPM, gain 0.25, same I–V–vi–IV loop and combo intensity layer. It should sit *under* the game, never compete with it.
