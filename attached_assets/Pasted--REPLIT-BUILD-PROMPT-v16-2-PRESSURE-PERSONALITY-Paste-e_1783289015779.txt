## 3 — Hearts (stakes without elimination)

- Player gets **3 hearts**, drawn (not emoji) under the score. Being chomped costs a heart and an escalating score steal: 1st = 25%, 2nd = 35%, 3rd = 50%.
- At 0 hearts: **FINAL HEART state** — a thin red pulsing vignette, steals against the player double, and the ticker fires "The void is wounded!" No elimination ever — comeback stays possible; the drama is the point.
- Hearts reset each round; bots have hearts too (same rules) so late-game hunting has texture.
