## 0 — Sprite collision fix (highest priority)

- On loading any sprite, scan its alpha channel once and compute the **tight bounding box** of non-transparent pixels; store it. All rendering scales use that trimmed box, so padding never inflates visuals.
- **Collision, absorb, suction, and "too big" push radii come ONLY from the CONFIG type-size table** — never from image dimensions. Audit every place a radius is computed and remove any reference to sprite width/height.
- Result: you can brush right up against a house's visible edge before anything pushes back. Test with the tree and both houses.
- Audit and remove the oversized faint ring currently rendering around the player (visible in live build) — every ring/aura must hug its owner's visual edge (max +10%).
- Minor: nothing may render on top of the evolution pill (a gnome sprite currently overlaps it) — HUD draws last, always.

- **FINAL FEAST — the last 30 seconds of every round:** banner + horn at 0:30, a subtle gold tint pulses at the screen edges, every void gets +10% speed, void-steals double to 50% for the duration, and small-object respawns taper to zero at 0:20.
