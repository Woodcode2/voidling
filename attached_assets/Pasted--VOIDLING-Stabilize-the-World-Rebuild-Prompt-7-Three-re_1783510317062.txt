# VOIDLING: Stabilize the World (Rebuild Prompt 7)
Three render defects are making the world look glitchy: buildings drift back and forth, some structures float above the ground, and the void has a white halo around it. Fix only these three. Diagnose the cause of each before changing anything.

## GOAL
A world that holds still. Buildings and structures are static, everything sits flush on the ground, and the void has no white ring. Nothing else changes.

## DO NOT TOUCH (leave fully intact)
- All gameplay, scoring, eating, placement counts, spawners, art, and the Prompt 1 through 6 work.
- Camera, ground, roads, river, waterfall, the win math.
- Do not swap any art or add anything. This is bug repair only.

## STAGE 0: Diagnose first and report the ROOT CAUSE of each (no fixes yet)
This is the most important stage. Do not fix anything until you have reported the actual cause of all three:
1. Buildings and structures appear to drift or oscillate back and forth in place. Find what applies per-frame motion to static structures. Is an idle bob or body-language animation wrongly applied to buildings? Is the depth sort recomputing positions and causing jitter? Is a position value being recalculated each frame? Report the exact cause.
2. Some structures and props render floating above the ground instead of sitting on it. Find the ground-anchor or height calculation for structures. Report why some sit high.
3. There is a white ring or halo drawn around the void body. Find where it is drawn. Report the draw call.
Paste all three root causes, then proceed.

## STAGE 1: Buildings and structures hold still
- Remove whatever is causing static structures to drift or oscillate. Buildings, houses, and scenery must not move on their own. The only motion a structure ever has is the swallow animation at the moment it is eaten, nothing else.
- If an idle animation layer is being applied to structures by mistake, scope it so it only applies to the things meant to move (people, vehicles), never to buildings, houses, or scenery.

## STAGE 2: Everything sits on the ground
- Every structure and prop anchors bottom-center at ground level, sitting flush on the surface. Nothing floats above it. Fix the anchor or height calc found in Stage 0 so the whole set sits correctly.

## STAGE 3: Remove the white void halo
- Remove the white ring drawn around the void. If you want to keep the void readable against the ground, use a soft dark contact shadow beneath it, never a white outline or glow.

## STAGE 4: Proof (real receipts)
Run a live match and report:
- The three root causes from Stage 0, and exactly what you changed for each.
- Watch the board for ten seconds with the void still: confirm no building, house, or scenery item drifts or oscillates.
- Confirm nothing floats above the ground.
- Confirm the void has no white ring.
- All audits still 0. Frame rate still in band. No new console errors.

If you could not find a root cause, say so instead of guessing at a fix. Do not self-certify.

- Open the preview and hold still. Buildings should be dead static, no back-and-forth.
- Everything should sit on the ground, nothing hovering.
- The void should have no white halo around it.
